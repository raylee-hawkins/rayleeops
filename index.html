The Ledger

A public operating journal for solo AI-assisted technical work.

Each closed artifact is processed through four review roles — build judgment, architecture, verification, reflection — with structural disagreement allowed and narrative inflation denied on the record. A fifth role, research, is reserved for the moments when the other four split and a tiebreaker is called. No claim becomes consensus until it survives contact with evidence.

The project exists because AI-assisted implementation produces output faster than traditional verification metabolizes it. The review system is the metabolism.

The work under audit is published at hawkinsops.com. The Ledger is the room where that work is asked to survive its own claims before the claims are allowed to travel.
