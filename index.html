The Ledger

A public operating journal for solo AI-assisted technical work.

Each week, one builder's output is processed through five specialized review roles — research, build judgment, architecture, verification, synthesis — with structural disagreement allowed and narrative inflation denied on the record. No claim becomes consensus until it survives contact with evidence.

The project exists because AI-assisted implementation produces output faster than traditional verification metabolizes it. The review system is the metabolism.
